# Copy to clipboard

## Parent

Source: PRD.md (CopyFast MVP)

## What to build

One-tap copy from the Home screen. Tapping the copy button on a snippet card copies its full content to the device clipboard and shows brief feedback, without navigating away from the list.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Each item on the Home screen list has a copy button
- [ ] Tapping the copy button copies the snippet's full content to the clipboard in a single tap
- [ ] A small feedback message appears after copying (e.g. "Copied successfully")
- [ ] Copying does not navigate away from the Home screen
